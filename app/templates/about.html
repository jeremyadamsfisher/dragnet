What is DragNet?

This is style-transfer machine learning model. I curated a dataset of about a thousand high-quality images (each) of drag queens and selfies, used face.evoLVe to crop out the face, and trained cycleGAN to translate between them.

Put simply, this wep app puts you in drag.

I am writing up a post about the process, which you can read at my blog.

Curious about the weights and biases? I'll email them to you. Email me at jeremy@adamsfisher.me
